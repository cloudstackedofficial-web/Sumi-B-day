Chapter 5

An envelope with one final thing to say.

Tap the seal when you are ready for the part I could never say in just one line.

My love,

Happy Birthday to the girl who made ordinary days feel beautiful without even trying. When I look at all these pictures, I do not just see places or dates. I see a thousand little versions of us laughing, roaming around, making messes, eating somewhere random, riding through the city, and somehow turning all of it into something unforgettable.

I still remember the Funzone day, the scooty rides, the beach days, the temple visits, the awkward camera angles, the shopping days, the hard phases, and the soft ones too. Some memories are loud, some are quiet, but all of them matter to me because they have you in them.

Thank you for being part of my life in a way that changed it. Thank you for the warmth, the patience, the chaos, the comfort, and the feeling that even a simple day can become special. I hope this year gives you peace, success, laughter, and every beautiful thing your heart deserves.

And no matter how many birthdays pass, I hope you never forget this one truth: you are deeply loved, endlessly admired, and remembered in more ways than words can fully hold.

Always yours.
